# Agriculture AI — Product Overview

> **Status**: Scoping in progress. App name, primary persona, and core workflow to be confirmed with product owner. Details marked TBD below will be filled in during the first-run A1 session.

---

## Product Concept

An AI-powered agriculture platform that helps farmers, consultants, and agri-businesses make smarter, data-driven decisions across crop management, field diagnostics, pest monitoring, and operational planning — delivering expert-level guidance to operators on the ground.

---

## Problem

Farmers and agricultural operators face complex, time-sensitive decisions — crop health monitoring, pest and disease detection, irrigation timing, weather-informed planting, yield forecasting — without access to actionable real-time intelligence. Traditional approaches are:

- Reactive and gut-driven, not data-led
- Dependent on scarce, expensive agronomic expertise
- Siloed across tools that don't talk to each other
- Costly when wrong: late interventions drive avoidable yield losses

---

## Solution

A web-based AI platform that turns field observations, imagery, and weather data into clear, actionable recommendations — so every operator acts with the confidence of an expert. The platform covers crop diagnostics, early-warning alerts, irrigation guidance, yield prediction, and a contextual AI advisory interface.

---

## Target Users (provisional)

| Persona | Role | Primary Need |
|---|---|---|
| **Farm Operator** | Day-to-day field management | Fast, reliable decisions on crop health and inputs |
| **Agronomist / Consultant** | Multi-farm advisory | Scalable monitoring and client-facing recommendations |
| **Agri-business Manager** | Multi-site operations oversight | Unified visibility and operational accountability |

Primary persona: **Farm Operator** (daily active user; others are secondary).

---

## Core Capabilities (provisional)

1. **Crop Health Diagnostics** — AI analysis of symptoms and field imagery to identify diseases and stress factors early
2. **Pest & Disease Alerts** — Early-warning notifications before outbreaks spread, with recommended interventions
3. **Irrigation Guidance** — Weather-informed watering schedules that reduce waste and protect crops from stress
4. **Yield Prediction** — Harvest forecasting from current field signals to inform planning and procurement
5. **Operations Log** — Record and track field activities, interventions, and observations in one place
6. **AI Advisory Chat** — On-demand expert-level agronomic guidance, 24/7

---

## Verified Operation (North Star)

**Crop Assessment Completed** — a field observation submitted through the app that results in an AI recommendation generated and reviewed by the operator. This is the core value event the platform exists to deliver; all volume and ROI projections are anchored to it.

## Strategic Principles

1. Every recommendation is traceable to real data and stated assumptions — no black-box magic
2. Mobile-ready for field use; dashboard view for planning and review
3. Plain-language output — operators are the audience, not agronomists
4. Every platform interaction produces a documented, reviewable outcome (assessment log)
5. Assumptions are always labeled; projections never masquerade as measured facts
