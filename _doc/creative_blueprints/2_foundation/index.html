Manual crop monitoring and reactive pest management

The Problem

Farmers face significant challenges in crop management, including unpredictable pest outbreaks, suboptimal irrigation scheduling, and difficulty predicting yields. Manual scouting is time-consuming, expensive, and often arrives too late to prevent losses.

The Solution

Traditional agriculture relies on manual field inspections and reactive responses to pest and disease outbreaks, leading to crop losses, inefficient resource use, and unpredictable yields.

Target Audience

Farm managers, agronomists, and agricultural enterprises managing 50+ acres

Personal Motivation

Empower farmers with data-driven decisions to increase yields while reducing input costs and environmental impact.
